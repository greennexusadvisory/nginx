Who we are

GNA is a Tirana-based expert collective specialized in strategic consulting at the intersection of governance, environment, and energy. Founded by senior adviser Dr. Elton Qendro (20+ years experience), we support governments, donors, NGOs, and academia with evidence-based, impact-oriented work.
