Who we are

Green Nexus Advisory is a Tirana-based expert collective specialized in strategic consulting at the intersection of governance, environment, and energy. Founded by senior adviser Dr. Elton Qendro (20+ years of experience), GNA supports governments, donors, NGOs, and academic institutions.

We bring applied knowledge in policy development, institutional analysis, climate security, and decentralized governance— focused on measurable results, inclusive processes, and strengthened public institutions across sectors.
