Who we are

Mission

Green Nexus Advisory is a Tirana-based consultancy supporting public institutions, donors, NGOs, and academia to improve governance outcomes across environment, energy, and local development. We combine policy analysis, stakeholder engagement, and delivery support to produce actionable, implementable results.

Evidence-based Donor-ready deliverables Inclusive processes

Approach

  • Policy design and alignment support (EU & national frameworks)
  • Stakeholder mapping, facilitation, and public consultation
  • Monitoring, evaluation, and learning (baseline → impact)
  • Capacity-building: tools, templates, and practical training

Services offered

Environmental & Climate Governance

  • Environmental policy design and reform
  • Support for EU alignment and implementation planning
  • Climate adaptation, risk governance, and compliance support
  • Environmental crime prevention and institutional coordination

Energy Transition & Policy Support

  • NECP review and policy gap analysis
  • Renewables policy support and stakeholder engagement
  • Socio-environmental assessment for energy infrastructure
  • Consultation design and facilitation for energy projects

Local Governance & Institutional Capacity

  • Decentralization and local governance support
  • Local public finance strategies (incl. royalties, budgeting)
  • Municipal service improvement frameworks
  • Training modules and capacity-building programmes

Research, Evaluation & Project Support

  • Baseline / midline / endline evaluations
  • Stakeholder perception studies and policy research
  • Monitoring frameworks and performance reviews
  • Concept notes, ToRs, and programme design support
